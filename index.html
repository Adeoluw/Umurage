CURRENT PROGRAMS

Basic English Tutoring

Fostering the culture of reading

Basic ICT tutoring

Social Skill development

Children are involved in social activities to develop and maintain positive interactions and increase their academic performance

OUR 4 pILLARS

Education

Our extra education programs help children to be more engaged in their education and are equipped with essential life skills

Health & Wellness

The center provides a safe and nurturing environment to disadvantaged children facing particular problems

Skill Building

Children build new skills that will help them achieve their goals and increase their chance of success

Recreation

The recreational activities allow children to have fun, broaden their ambitions, and develop skills such as understanding, thinking, teamwork, and discipline

OUR MISSION

Who we are

We are a community-driven organization that develops programs based on the needs of children and youth in the rural communities we serve

Our Mission

Our mission is to help Rwandan children from impoverished families break the cycle of poverty and fulfill their potential through programs in education, health and wellness, skill-building, and recreation

Our Goal

The overall goal of Umurage community center is to bring change to children and youth in the rural communities we serve through integrated programs in education, health & wellness, skill-building, and recreational activities

Our vision

Our vision is to create a world where children and youth from poor families are empowered with meaningful skills to be able to thrive and reach their full potential
